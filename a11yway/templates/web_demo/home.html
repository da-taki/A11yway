Evidence and reporting

Separate passive security observations

Optional header and public-page hygiene observations. These are not part of the accessibility score and are not vulnerability testing.

Required before an audit can start. The demo only reviews public pages and never submits public forms.

No API keys or local filesystem paths are sent to browser JavaScript.

Reports

  • Strong findingsGrouped with severity, WCAG, selectors, snippets, and fixes.
  • Needs reviewKept separate when confidence requires a human call.
  • ArtifactsScreenshots, focus paths, and machine-readable outputs stay linked.

Supported checks

Serious coverage, loud wrapper

Structure and semantics

Headings, landmarks, labels, language, alt text, and HTML patterns are checked from fetched source.

Forms and controls

Inputs, grouping, instructions, errors, and recovery patterns are reviewed without submitting forms.

Keyboard navigation

Focus traversal, repeated stops, lost focus, and focus-loop risks are recorded when browser evidence runs.

Accessibility-tree evidence

Rendered DOM and computed announcements support reviewer-visible evidence, not just source inspection.

Low vision and reflow

Contrast, focus visibility, zoom reflow, mobile overflow, and touch target signals are surfaced.

Language and Indic scripts

Multilingual, bidirectional, and Indic-language markup signals are prepared for human follow-up.

Developer reports

HTML, Markdown, JSON, CSV, SARIF, and JUnit outputs support teams, judges, and CI workflows.

AI Scout

Suggest-only summaries prioritize deterministic findings without independently establishing failures.

Safety and scope

Responsible public testing only

A11yway does not log in, submit forms, test payments, scan networks, bypass authentication, exploit weaknesses, or claim legal compliance certification.

  • Public pages only
  • No private systems
  • No form submissions
  • No security exploitation
  • Human review required
  • No legal certification claim
